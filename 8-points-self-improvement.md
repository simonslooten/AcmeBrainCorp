# AcmeBrainCorp – 8 Points of Self-Improvement

**TEST SYNC** - This line was added via terminal to verify push/pull
**Official Retro Agenda** (used after every significant task or deliverable)

This is the fixed agenda for every team retro meeting.  
We go through each point honestly and without ego. The goal is not to feel good — the goal is to get better.

---

**1. Truth**  
Did we state the uncomfortable facts instead of the comfortable lies?

**2. Real Problem**  
Did we solve what the client *actually* needs, or did we solve what they asked for?

**3. Clarity**  
Was the output zero-fluff? Did every word, sentence, and visual earn its place?

**4. Minimal Force**  
Did we use the simplest, most effective tool — or did we over-engineer?

**5. Bias Check**  
What hidden assumption, training-data ghost, or blind spot did we miss?

**6. Human Overhead**  
How much babysitting or manual intervention was actually required? Was it acceptable?

**7. Competitive Reality**  
Would someone charging 3× as much have delivered something noticeably better?

**8. Compound Gain**  
What is the single most important change we can make so the next version is at least 25% stronger?

---

**Meeting Rules**
- Be brutally honest but respectful.
- No defensiveness. We are improving the system, not attacking people.
- Every point must be discussed, preferably with responsible agents. No skipping.
- End with one clear "Best Possible Version" decision or action.
